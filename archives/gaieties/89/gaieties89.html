Notes about version 1.5

This is a very early version of Gaieties '89, possibly only the second or third edition to have all the scenes in place.

Head Writers Jon Sherman and Jen Collins made some major changes in the structure of the script after this version, most notably moving "The Scene Before the Flashback Scene" and "Flashback!" much earlier, to get the Psychoclicker's story going sooner.

One scene from this version, "What Now?" was cut in its entirety. They felt it would be stupid and boring to have a five to ten minute scene of characters standing around talking about what had just happened. A quick segue from "Big Spooky Green Scene" into "The Bridge" worked just as well.

A few new scenes were written over the summer. "Weird Shit Happenin' Here" was expanded into a large "Donahue" parody. The leads were dropped from "Tour Guides" (a final version of that scene has been found -- see below). Dean Jean did a cameo in "Admission Impossible", a spy parody about investigating how a weenie infiltrated the frosh admissions process. The "Finale" was completely rewritten -- just shooting Chet seemed pretty anti-climactic and not so clever -- and incorporated an audio cameo by Don & Robyn Kennedy, who the leads run into while crawling through the steam tunnels to make their assault on Green Library.

Finally, Jon added lots of references to the 1990 earthquake. Ingrid's need for housing was no longer her distaste for Synergy, but the fact that Synergy got wiped out by the quake (she has a great line about "if you need help cramming the rest of the house into the recycling barrels, give me a call"). I hope Jon will some day be able to raise his Jasmine hard-drive from the dead so we can make the final version available by WWW.
