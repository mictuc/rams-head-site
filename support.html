SUPPORT RAM'S HEAD


We are so grateful for the support we receive from our friends, family, and alumni. Thanks to many generous alumni, Ram's Head Theatrical Society is supported by the Ram's Head Endowment, initiated in 2012 and made possible in part through donations in memory of Laura Wilson ('96, Executive Producer 1995-1996 and Producer of Spring Show 1995: Once on this Island), who passed away in 2012 after her battle with cancer. This endowment gives Ram's Head financial security like never before and makes sure we are around for another 100 years of incredible performances, valuable opportunities that empower students, and unforgettable friendships and memories.

If you would like to make a donation to the Ram's Head Endowment, follow these steps:
1. Visit giving.stanford.edu
2. Click the "Make a Gift" button on the far right
3. Go to the drop down menu and select "Centers, Institutes, and More"
4. Choose "Other Designation (Specify Below)"
