SUPPORT RAM'S HEAD


We are so grateful for the support we receive from our friends, family, and alumni. Thanks to many generous alumni, Ram's Head Theatrical Society is supported by the Ram's Head Endowment, initiated in 2012 and made possible in part through donations in memory Laura Wilson ('96, Executive Producer 1995-1996 and Producer of Spring Show 1995: Once on this Island), who passed away in 2012 after her battle with cancer. This endowment gives Ram's Head financial security like never before and make sure we are around for another 100 years of incredible performances, valuable opportunities that empower students, and unforgettable friendships and memories.

If you would like to make a donation to the Ram's Head Endowment, visit giving.stanford.edu, choose to support "Centers, Institutes, and More", select "Other Designation", and write in "Ram's Head Theatrical Society Endowment Fund". Alternatively, you may donate by mail. Please make your check out to Stanford University and note on the check that it is for the Ram's Head Theatrical Society Endowment. Mail checks to:

Stanford University Office of Development
Attention: Julia Hartung
326 Galvez Street
Stanford, CA 94305

If you have any questions about the endowment, feel free to contact Alumni Board President .
