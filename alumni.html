RAM'S HEAD ALUMNI

Ram's Head Alumni Board

The Alumni Board was created in January 2013 with the purpose of:

  • Guiding and supporting the current Ram's Head leadership
  • Increasing the long-term stability and institutional memory of Ram's Head
  • Augmenting and strengthening Ram's Head's alumni network

Keep In Touch

Interested in seeing what we've been up to since you graduated? Want to reconnect with some of your Ram's Head classmates? Join our Alumni Mailing List, like us on Facebook, join our SAA group, and join our Facebook Alumni Group! Alumni event invitations and current Ram's Head happenings are posted to these places.

Support Ram's Head

We are so grateful for the support we receive from our alumni. Thanks to many generous alumni, Ram's Head Theatrical Society is supported by the Ram's Head Endowment, initiated in 2012 and made possible in part through donations in memory Laura Wilson ('96, Executive Producer 1995-1996 and Producer of Spring Show 1995: Once on this Island), who passed away in 2012 after her battle with cancer. This endowment gives Ram's Head financial security like never before and make sure we are around for another 100 years of incredible performances, valuable opportunities that empower students, and unforgettable friendships and memories.

For instructions on how to help support Ram's Head, visit our Support Ram's page.
